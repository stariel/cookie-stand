About Pat's Salmon Cookies

Pat's Salmon Cookies are a delicious accompaniment to coffee or tea. Eat them at any time of day for a healthy snack rich in protein and omega3 fatty acids. Our patented microgrinding process eliminates fish taste while retaining the health benefits of salmon.
